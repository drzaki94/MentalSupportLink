🫁 Box Breathing

Breathe in deeply for 4 seconds. Hold your breath for 4 seconds. Breathe out slowly for 4 seconds. Pause for 4 seconds. Repeat 4 times. Tarik nafas dalam-dalam selama 4 saat. Tahan nafas selama 4 saat. Hembus perlahan-lahan selama 4 saat. Berhenti seketika selama 4 saat. Ulang 4 kali.
